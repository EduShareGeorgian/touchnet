Sixth annual Georgian College Orillia Campus-wide Career Fair, 825 Memorial Avenue, Orillia ON, Wednesday, March 12, 2014, 10am - 2pm in the Gym.

Hosted by Career and Co-op Services, this event allows employers the chance to share information about their organization and network with current students and graduates seeking information about their career choices and employment opportunities. Last year's event attracted 500 students/attendees visiting with close to 40 employers. Help transform education into inspired careers.

Registration deadline is February 20, 2014.
